Merapi Lava Tour

Kaliurang, Yogyakarta

About the Travel

The Lava Tour of Merapi is a jeep ride around the Merapi area which was affected by the Merapi eruption in 2010. The Merapi Lava Tour is a must-visit tourist spot in Jogja because the location of the city of Jogja is very close, only 20 minutes away. Besides that, it is also because the Merapi lava tour is a cheap Jogja tourist spot, affordable for all people.

Besides being close to the city of Jogja, Merapi Lava Tour is a tourist spot in Jogja near Malioboro. If you visit Malioboro, there is nothing wrong with visiting the Kaliurang Lava Tour which is only 20 minutes from Malioboro.
